I am currently a senior SDE at AWS, leading the client metrics team for Amazon Chime and Wickr.

My academic work focuses on researching and developing tools and techniques for simulating and analyzing complex systems. In particular, my work focuses on extending the capabilities of traditional agent-based modeling, analyzing agent-based models with machine learning, and incorporating machine learning techniques into the behaviors of agents.
